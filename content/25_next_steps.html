Start creating a portfolio

There are two huge benefits of creating a portfolio:

  • You get to practice your new skills on practical projects.
  • You'll have stuff to show potential employers or clients.

There's more than one way to create a portfolio, and here are two good choices:

  • Show off your mini projects on a site like codepen.
  • Create a full site and show your projects there.

Portfolio project ideas:

Feel free to use as few or as many of these suggestions as you want.

  • Basic form validation
  • A useful popup (settings, a sign-up form, more info, etc)
  • Some sort of calculation helper (maybe a tip calculator, a tax calculator, a fitness/nutrition calculator, etc)
  • A quiz game
  • Rock, paper, scissors
  • A navigation menu that expands when you click
  • An interactive pizza menu where you can add toppings, sides, beverages, etc, and get the final price and order summary

As an added bonus, try making something related to the industry you're interested in. Examples:

  • Interested in gaming? Make a small game.
  • Finance? Make an investment calculator.
  • Restaurants? Make an interactive menu (build your own burger or catering order for example).
  • Cars? Make a car customization page where people can pick out features and get a total price.
  • Fashion? Make a pick-your-own-outfit page.
  • Website creation? Create a project estimation calculator where people can pick features they want on their websites and get a price quote.

If you ever get stuck, you can refer back to any part of this course.

Best of luck!
